Selected Projects

Project 01 — Featured
ParcelScan — Damaged Packaging Detection
EfficientNetV2B0 · YOLOv8 · TFLite · Android · TensorFlow
End-to-end warehouse demo application detecting damaged packaging in real time. Two-model pipeline: a fine-tuned EfficientNetV2B0 classifier and YOLOv8n object detector, both quantized and exported to TFLite for on-device Android inference. Custom 500-image dataset augmented to 350 per class, with INT8 quantization producing a ~20 MB model. Custom NMS logic for the YOLOv8 [1, 84, 8400] output tensor implemented in Android.
Project 02
Bayesian Classification on Cardiotocogram Data
Python · Bayesian Learning · scikit-learn
Applied Sequential Bayesian Learning with Gaussian basis functions. Achieved 51.6% faster training versus a ridge regression baseline on the Cardiotocogram medical dataset.

Independent Research

Ongoing
2025 — Present
Independent · Remote
DNABERT-2 Genomic Analysis & Clustering
Transformers · Bioinformatics · HuggingFace · Python
Investigating the capability of DNABERT-2 — a pre-trained transformer model for genomic sequences — to generate contextual embeddings from human genome data. Applying unsupervised clustering (K-Means, UMAP) to identify functional and structural patterns across genomic regions. This work extends a long-standing interest in bioinformatics, rooted in an undergraduate thesis on alignment-free phylogeny reconstruction, into the era of large language models applied to biological sequences.
